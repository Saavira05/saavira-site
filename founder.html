About the Founder

Saavira was founded by Haard Joshi, a hotelier and aviator with a passion for creativity and thoughtful design.

While working in the hospitality industry, he observed how many women struggled to find outfits that balanced everyday comfort with elegant style. Inspired by this gap, he decided to step away from his career to build something meaningful.

This vision led to the creation of Saavira – Your Everyday Style, a brand dedicated to simple, graceful, and versatile ethnic wear for modern women.
