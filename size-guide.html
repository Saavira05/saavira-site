Size Guide

Measurements are body measurements (not garment). If between sizes, we suggest choosing the larger size for comfort. This is a general reference; styles and fabrics may vary.

How to Measure

  • Bust: Measure around the fullest part of your chest.
  • Waist: Measure around the natural waistline.
  • Hip: Measure around the fullest part of your hips.
  • Kurti length: From shoulder to desired hem.
