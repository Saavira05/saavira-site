About Saavira

Saavira was created with a simple idea: fashion should be effortless, comfortable, and elegant enough to be worn every day.

In a world where trends change quickly, we believe in timeless style that fits naturally into everyday life. Saavira focuses on modern kurtis designed for women who want to feel confident, comfortable, and stylish at the same time.

Each piece is thoughtfully designed to combine simplicity, elegance, and practicality—perfect for work, casual outings, or daily wear.

About the Founder

Saavira was founded by Haard Joshi, a hotelier and aviator with a passion for creativity and thoughtful design.

While working in the hospitality industry, he observed how many women struggled to find outfits that balanced everyday comfort with elegant style.

Inspired by this gap, he stepped away from his career to create Saavira – Your Everyday Style, a brand dedicated to simple, graceful, and versatile ethnic wear for modern women.
